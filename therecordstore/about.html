About Us:

Shayna's Record Store was started with alternative music lovers at the forefront of the idea.
We are dedicated to providing a unique shopping experience for vinyl enthusiasts and collectors.

Our store offers a wide selection of vinyl records, including both current releases and classic albums. We also have a knowledgeable staff that is passionate about music and can help you find the perfect record for your collection.

If you're looking for a specific album or artist, feel free to ask our staff for assistance. We are always happy to help our customers find what they're looking for.
Feel free to browse or stop in, we'd love to see you!
